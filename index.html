Our Story

A Legend Born from
Culinary Passion

The GOAT Restaurant represents the pinnacle of fine dining in Chennai—where Michelin-star artistry meets luxurious ambiance. Founded in 2018 by Chef Arjun Krishnan after two decades of mastering his craft across Paris, Tokyo, and Mumbai, The GOAT was conceived as a singular vision: to create a dining experience so extraordinary that every guest leaves feeling legendary.

Our culinary philosophy is rooted in the sacred tension between perfection and passion. We source only the finest ingredients—A5 Wagyu from Japan, Norwegian salmon, 45-day aged prime ribeye—and pair them with rare Indian spices and traditional techniques reimagined for the modern palate. Every dish is a conversation between continents.

At The GOAT, we believe a truly great restaurant should stir the soul, not merely feed the body. Our promise to every guest is an experience of such beauty and excellence that you will measure all future meals against it.

Meet Chef Arjun Krishnan

Executive Chef & Culinary Director

Michelin-trained at Paris's Institut Paul Bocuse, Chef Arjun Krishnan has spent 20+ years mastering the art of fine dining across three continents—refining his craft at two-star establishments in Paris, studying kaiseki precision in Tokyo, and discovering the soul of his cooking in Mumbai's vibrant food culture.

Winner of 'Best Chef India 2024' and the youngest Indian chef ever to earn a Michelin star on French soil, Chef Arjun returned to his Tamil Nadu roots in 2018 to build The GOAT—his life's greatest work. His philosophy: every dish must make the guest feel legendary.
